# College School Management System  
## Packages, pricing & support

**All prices are quoted in Ghana Cedis (GHS).**  
This overview explains how our packages are structured, what each option includes, and how support works after you go live.

---

## Why we price this way

Your college pays an **annual licence** that matches both **what you need** (modules) and **how many students** you serve. That keeps costs fair: smaller institutions are not subsidising larger ones, and you only pay for the scope you use.

Each package covers **one campus** (one institution per installation).

**Active students** — For billing, we count students who are enrolled and active on the system for the academic year in question. Your formal quotation will state exactly how we measure this so there is no ambiguity.

## Choose your package

We offer three levels. Move up when you need finance, deeper administration, evaluations, and full system tools.

### Core Academic

*Ideal if you want structured academic records, timetabling, and results workflows, and will handle fees outside the system or separately.*

| Area | What you get |
|------|----------------|
| **Getting started** | Guided setup: school profile, faculties, departments, programmes, halls |
| **Academic structure** | Faculties, departments, programmes, courses, academic sessions and semesters, timetables |
| **Students** | Admissions (when open), approval process, student records, printable views |
| **Results & records** | Grade points, entering and uploading results, approval workflow, transcripts |
| **Student & teacher portals** | Students: courses, timetable, results, transcript, attendance (where enabled). Teachers: courses, timetable, class lists, attendance, assessments (where enabled) |
| **Teaching staff** | Teacher profiles and core assignment management |

*Not included at this level:* fee management and finance reports, advanced reporting suite, full HR-style staff administration, teacher evaluations, and some student-life modules listed under Professional and Complete.

---

### Professional

*Everything in Core Academic, plus finance, reporting, and day-to-day administration.*

| Area | What you get |
|------|----------------|
| **Finance** | Fee structures, recording payments, outstanding balances, scholarships and grants |
| **Reports** | Academic, payment, and attendance reports |
| **People & roles** | Administrative staff, non-teaching staff, staff and teacher assignments, role management |
| **Communication** | Teacher announcements and course materials review (as released in your version) |
| **Student journey** | Promotion and graduation, medical records, disciplinary records (staff and student views where applicable) |
| **Clearance** | Departmental clearance tracking |

---

### Complete

*Everything in Professional, plus evaluations and advanced system administration.*

| Area | What you get |
|------|----------------|
| **Teacher evaluations** | Build forms, schedule evaluations, collect student responses, reporting |
| **Security & administration** | Passport-style photo checks for profiles, user accounts, roles and permissions, backup and restore tools, system configuration |

Optional **priority support** (faster response times, named contact) can be added to any package — ask when you request a quote.

### Alternative: per-student pricing

Some colleges prefer a single formula:

**Annual fee = (Number of active students × rate per student per year)**, with a **minimum annual fee** so very small cohorts remain sustainable.

## Your first year: support & updates (included)

**Every package includes twelve months of care** from your agreed start date (usually go-live). During this period you receive:

| Benefit | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Help with defects** | We fix faults in the features covered by your package, in line with fair response times stated in your agreement. |
| **Product updates** | Improvements and enhancements to the features you already licensed — delivered as updates across the year. |
| **How to reach us** | Channels such as email and agreed business-hours support (exact channels are in your contract). |
| **Release notes** | Summary of changes when we ship updates. |

**Not included in standard support:** bespoke reports outside the product, integrations with third-party systems unless purchased, on-site visits unless agreed, or correcting data that was entered incorrectly by your team.

## After the first year

### Staying current — annual renewal

To **keep using the software**, receive **ongoing updates**, and **continue support** for your licensed features, your college renews each year. Renewal is typically a **fixed annual amount** or a **percentage of the current list price** for your package and size — your contract will state the method and amount.

We will explain clearly what renewal includes and what happens if you choose not to renew, before you sign.

### New features & custom work

**Add-ons and upgrades** — Moving to a higher package (for example from Professional to Complete) is priced as an upgrade.

**Bespoke work** — New features built only for your college, or connections to SMS, mobile money, accounting packages, or other systems, are **scoped and quoted separately**. You approve the scope and price before work begins.

> *New capabilities that are not part of your current package are quoted as separate projects. Your existing licence continues to cover everything already included.*
